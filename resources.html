Recommended Books

Here are some highly recommended math books that can supplement your learning and help you strengthen your understanding of various math concepts:

  • Mathematics: A Complete Introduction by David Glover
  • Calculus Made Easy by Silvanus P. Thompson
  • The Princeton Companion to Mathematics edited by Timothy Gowers
  • Algebra: Pure and Applied by Aigli Papantonopoulou
  • Geometry: A Comprehensive Course by Dan Pedoe

Online Resources
